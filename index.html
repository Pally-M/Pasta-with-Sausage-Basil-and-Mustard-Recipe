Pasta with Sausage Basil and Mustard

In matching spicy sausage with a creamy mustard sauce and fragrant basil, British food with warm, mildy spicy flavours.

Active Time:

10 mins

Total Time:

20 mins

Yield:

4 servings

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound penne, rigatoni, or medium shells
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • 8 hot Italian sausages, meat removed from casings and crumbled (about 1 1/2 pounds)
  • 3/4 cup dry white wine

Directions

Step 1

  1. Gather all the ingredients

Step 2

  1. Cook the pasta in a large pot of boiling salted water unil al dente and then drain

Step 3

  1. Meanwhile, heat the olive oil in a large, deep skillet. Add the sausage meat and brown over moderately high heat, about 5 minutes

Step 4

  1. Add the wine and simmer, scraping up the browned bits from the bottom, until reduced by half, about 5 minutes.

Step 5

  1. Add the cream, mustard, and crushed red pepper and simmer for 2 minutes.

Step 6

  1. Remove the skillet from the heat, add the pasta and basil, and toss to coat

Step 7

  1. Serve at once
